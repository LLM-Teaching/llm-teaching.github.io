Welcome to SynthEddix

SynthEddix is a project exploring how Large Language Models can be integrated into learning environments for interactive, customizable teaching experiences.

Project Aim

Our goal is to build a system that allows educators to easily curate and adapt study content with the help of LLMs. The tailored material generation is designed with flexibility and usability in mind. ...

Who We Are

This project is currently a collaboration between Mathilda Buschmann, Cornelius Wolff, and Leonie Grafweg, in partnership with the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI). Our interdisciplinary team wants to bridge AI and education to create innovative learning tools. ...
